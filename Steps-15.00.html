Grouping Air crash Years by Decade 15
Steps:
  1. Right click on Year under the Calendar Table.
  2. Once the dropdown appears, click on New Group, and a Groups window will appear
  3. Here we will be able to group the years by decade so that the visual is easier to view than having over 100 years of data individually shown by each specific year. Within this window we will change to Group Type to List instead of Bins.
  4.  In order to group them, click each year that you want grouped while holding the CTRL key on the keyboard.
  5.  We will be grouping the years into decades, for the first group select year 1918 â€“ 1919, these will be the 1910s.
  6.  Once selected press the Group button towards the bottom.
  7.  Once selected, a group will appear on the right-hand side box under Groups and Members. Double clicks the new group and rename it to 1910s
  8.  Repeat this process for all the decades, there should be 12 decades in total
