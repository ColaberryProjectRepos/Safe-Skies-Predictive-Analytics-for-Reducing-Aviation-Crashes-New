Model Performance:

The ExtraTreesClassifier demonstrated outstanding performance, as shown by the Receiver Operating Characteristic (ROC) curves for each class, all of which achieved an area under the curve (AUC) of 0.99. This high AUC reflects a strong true positive rate with minimal false positives, underscoring the model's accuracy and reliability in multi-class classification tasks. These results indicate that the model is particularly effective in distinguishing between crash and no-crash scenarios, making it a reliable tool for practical use in aviation safety applications.


Visualizations:

This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of air crash data spanning from 1918 to 2022. By investigating incidents and examining factors such as flight phases and crash causes, the dashboard reveals critical patterns and trends. It highlights key risk areas by tracking fatality rates and their year-over-year trends, as well as changes in survival rates. Additionally, it offers insights into total crashes per year and fatalities per crash. The goal is to enhance aviation safety by simplifying complex data, thereby supporting more informed safety practices and decision-making for the future.
