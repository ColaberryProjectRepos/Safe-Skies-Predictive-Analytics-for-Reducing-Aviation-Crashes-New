Creating and Formatting Map Visual 23
 Steps:  
  1.   Right click on the Crashes by Flight Phase visual and copy paste (CTRL+V) 
  2. Click on the map from the visualization pane
Build Visual Tab: 
  • Location= Country, Bubble size= Crashes per Year and place all the KPIs on the tooltip
Format Visual Tab:
  • Visual:  Map Setting Style= Grayscale, Controls: Auto Zoom=on, Zoom buttons=ON, Bubbles size =3 Color Conditional Formatting Gradient Field. YOY% Crashes stay Lowest #A0D1FF Middle #41a4ff Highest # 094780, Category Labels =OFF
  • General: Properties Size Height= 332 Width= 362, Position Horizontal=917, Vertical= 387, Title =ON, Text = Crashes by Country
