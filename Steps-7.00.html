Visualizations: Case 2: Objective: Enhance Pilot Training to Reduce Human Error-Related Crashes 7

Positive Insight:

Reduction in Crashes During Landing:

Positive Insight:

  • Reduction in Crashes During Landing:
    Between 1918-2022, 9,856 were crashes due to human errors, 4,997 occurred during landing. Enhanced pilot training can reduce these landing-related crashes by 35%, significantly improving safety and addressing the 3.8% survivor rate to enhance passenger safety.

Negative Insight:

  • High Incidence of Human Error:

 Human error plays a major role in aviation safety, contributing to 60% of all crashes.  The severity of these incidents is highlighted by a high fatality rate of 7.4% and a low survivor rate of just 3.8%, which is decreasing Year-over-Year. By enhancing pilot training, we can significantly improve safety outcomes, reducing both the frequency and severity of crashes.


ROI:
Implementing advanced pilot training programs can save operators $3 million annually by reducing crash-related costs, including aircraft damage, compensation, and legal fees. With an $800,000   investment in pilot training programs, the ROI is 375%, highlighting the substantial financial benefits of investing in enhanced pilot training.
